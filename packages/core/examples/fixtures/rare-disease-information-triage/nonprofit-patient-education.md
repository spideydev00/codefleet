## Patient-friendly overview

Some rare neuromuscular conditions can cause exercise intolerance, proximal muscle weakness, and elevated CK. Patients often describe a long search for answers and may compare their symptoms with community stories.

## Simplified explanation

A patient who has progressive muscle fatigue, difficulty climbing stairs, and elevated CK may want to discuss rare muscle disease possibilities with a clinician. In patient communities, symptom overlap can make several rare conditions appear similar.

## Important limitation

This educational content is simplified for readability. Similar symptoms do not identify one disease. Many rare and non-rare conditions can share overlapping muscle symptoms.

## Suggested non-diagnostic use

Patients may use this information to prepare questions for a neuromuscular specialist, organize a symptom timeline, and ask whether genetic counseling or further evaluation is appropriate.
